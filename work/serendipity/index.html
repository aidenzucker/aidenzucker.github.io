Serendipity.how
Experimental website and tool for thinking about serendipity in a new way
PROJECT QUESTION
How might creative problem solvers think differently about innovation and creativity?
serendipity.how
To show people the joy of unexpected discoveries, we made a tool for making weird juxtapositions and sharing them with your friends.
There are currently 111 images in the database, which are surfaced randomly every time the page loads. Images were included with the following criteria:
  • Singularity of the image
  • Multiple and metaphorical interpretations
  • Aesthetically and emotionally compelling
