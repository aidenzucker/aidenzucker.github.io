Aiden is a fourth-year communication design student at the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts at Washington University in St. Louis. Most recently, he developed a set of bilingual design guidelines and tools for Start-Up Chile, the largest startup community in the world. Previously, he worked as a design intern for IBM Design in Austin, Texas and a project supervisor for Amigos de las Américas in Cajamarca, Peru. Outside of design, he enjoys speaking Spanish, playing soccer, backpacking, and puns.
